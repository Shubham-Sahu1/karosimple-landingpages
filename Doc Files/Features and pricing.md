| A2[REQUIRED] | Yes. There is no minimum commitment on monthly plans. Cancel before your next billing date and you won't be charged again. For half-yearly and annual plans, you keep access until the period ends. |
| Q3[REQUIRED] | What's the difference between PRO and AGENCY? |
| A3[REQUIRED] | PRO is built for one business or up to 3 locations that you run yourself. AGENCY is for marketing agencies or franchise owners who manage multiple client businesses under one roof. | Q4[REQUIRED] | What does '10,000 AI credits pool' mean for Agency? |
| A4[REQUIRED] | Your agency account gets 10,000 AI review generation credits every month. You decide how many each client business gets.
